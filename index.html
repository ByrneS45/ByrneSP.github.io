Sean Byrne

Experimental Nuclear Physicist | USMC Veteran | Researcher in Nuclear Astrophysics

About Me

I’m a PhD candidate in Experimental Nuclear Physics at UMass Lowell, specializing in gamma-ray spectroscopy and nucleosynthesis. I began my journey in the USMC, developing a foundation of discipline and leadership, and transitioned into academic research after earning my B.S. in Physics and Biomolecular Science.

Research

My current work focuses on constraining reaction rates for explosive nucleosynthesis via (p,γ) reactions on neutron-deficient nuclei. I use experimental data and numerical modeling to refine astrophysical reaction networks.
